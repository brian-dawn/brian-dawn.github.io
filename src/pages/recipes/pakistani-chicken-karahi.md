## Ingredients

- 1 pound (450 grams) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into pieces
- Salt
- 1–2 tablespoons cooking oil or ghee
- 2 garlic cloves, crushed
- 1-inch piece fresh ginger, crushed
- 3 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- MSG, optional
- Green chilies, optional
- 1 tablespoon butter, optional
- Julienned ginger and chopped cilantro, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Season the chicken with salt. Heat the oil or ghee in a wide skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Sear the chicken until browned on the outside.
3. Add the garlic and ginger and cook briefly until fragrant.
4. Add the tomatoes and spices. Cook, stirring and crushing the tomatoes, until they form a thick sauce and the chicken is cooked through.
5. Finish with optional butter and green chilies. Adjust salt and spice to taste.
6. Garnish with ginger and cilantro. Serve with rice or flatbread.
