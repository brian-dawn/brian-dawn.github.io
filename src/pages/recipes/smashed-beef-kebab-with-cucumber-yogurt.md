## Ingredients

- 2 cups Greek yogurt
- 2 Kirby cucumbers, grated
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh mint, plus more for garnish
- 1 garlic clove, finely grated
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 small yellow onion, grated
- 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 2 teaspoons kosher salt, divided
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 1/4 cup walnuts
- 2 tablespoons raisins or dried cranberries
- Pomegranate molasses, for serving
- Warm pita, flatbread, or rice, for serving

## Instructions

1. Mix the yogurt, cucumber, mint, garlic, and half the salt. Refrigerate while cooking the beef.
2. Combine the beef, onion, turmeric, remaining salt, and plenty of black pepper.
3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat. Add the beef in several large, rough pieces and leave undisturbed until deeply browned and crisp underneath, about 6–8 minutes.
4. Break up the beef. Add the walnuts and raisins, then cook for another 2–3 minutes, until the beef is cooked and the walnuts are toasted.
5. Spread the cucumber yogurt on a platter. Top with the beef mixture, drizzle with pomegranate molasses, and garnish with mint.
6. Serve with warm pita, flatbread, or rice.
