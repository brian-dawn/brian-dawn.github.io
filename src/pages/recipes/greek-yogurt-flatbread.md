## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup self-rising flour
- Or: 1/2 cup gluten-free all-purpose flour, 3/4 teaspoon baking powder, and 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil, optional

## Optional Garlic Topping

- 1 teaspoon melted butter or olive oil
- 1 small garlic clove, minced
- Chopped parsley
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Mix the yogurt and flour until a rough dough forms. Add the optional olive oil if desired.
2. Knead for 1–2 minutes, until mostly smooth. Rest for 10 minutes if time allows.
3. Divide into two balls and roll each to about 1/8-inch thickness.
4. Cook in a hot, dry skillet for 2–3 minutes per side, until puffed in spots and golden.
5. Mix the garlic topping ingredients and brush over the warm flatbreads.
