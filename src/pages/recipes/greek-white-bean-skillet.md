## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 can (15 ounces) cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 3 cups baby spinach
- 1/3 cup crumbled feta
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 2 tablespoons chopped dill or parsley
- Salt and black pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for about 30 seconds.
2. Add the tomatoes and cook until softened and beginning to burst, about 4–5 minutes.
3. Stir in the beans and spinach. Cook until the beans are hot and the spinach has wilted.
4. Remove from the heat. Add the feta, lemon juice, and herbs. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve warm with whole-grain bread or flatbread.
